About Ozzyl Tools

Practical online tools built for speed, privacy, and clear decisions.

Ozzyl Tools is a free collection of business calculators, PDF tools, document generators, data utilities, and writing tools. The goal is to help people finish common tasks without a complicated dashboard, forced sign-up, or hidden workflow.

What we build

Small tools for real work

Ozzyl Tools helps freelancers, small-business owners, online sellers, service providers, students, and operations teams calculate business figures, work with documents and files, and complete common browser-based tasks.

Each calculator explains its formula and assumptions. Supported file tools are designed to work locally in the browser. Document generators provide a live preview and local draft saving.

Product principles

How Ozzyl Tools is designed

{{ icon('shield', 24) }}

Privacy-first

Calculator values, supported files, and document drafts are processed in the browser by default. Any future cloud feature must clearly explain when data is sent to a server.

{{ icon('info', 24) }}

Transparent

Formulas, assumptions, limitations, and local-rule warnings are shown near results. The tools provide practical estimates, not professional legal, tax, or accounting advice.

{{ icon('check', 24) }}

Halal-friendly

The public catalog excludes interest and usury calculations, mortgage-interest, debt-interest, compound-return tools, gambling, betting, adult content, intoxicants, and deceptive schemes.

Content standards

Useful before promotional

Pages are written to answer the task directly: what the tool does, how it works, what the result means, and what should be verified. We avoid fabricated testimonials, invented statistics, misleading guarantees, keyword stuffing, and link schemes.
